Who are we?

Since 1986, we have been part of the cultural and musical life of Benimaclet. Our school is a space dedicated to training, creativity, and passion for music, where generation of students hace discovered their artistic potencial in a welcoming, family-like enviroment.

With our three decades of experience, we offer classes for all ages and levels, from beginner to advanced, in instruments like piano, guitar, voilin, drums, wind instruments, and voice, as well as workshops in harmony, music theory, and choral and instrumental groups. Our team of profesional musicians with a strong teaching background adapts to the needs and motivation of each student, encourage both technical learning and enjoyment of music.

In addition to academic training we organize concerts, auditions, and communitiy activities that enrich the life of the neighbothood, keeping alive the musical lagacy that define us. At Benimaclet Music school, we don't just teach notes-we nuture emotions, connections, and a tradition that has lasted for nearly 40 years.
